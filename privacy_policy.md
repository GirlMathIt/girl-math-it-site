# Girl Math It Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12th, 2026.

Girl Math It is designed to be private and local-first. The app does not use accounts, servers, analytics, ads, or third-party tracking.

## Data Stored on Your Device

Girl Math It lets you save purchase names, paid amounts, use counts, categories, purchase dates, and optional item photos. This information is stored locally on your device using the app's local database and app document storage.

## Photos

If you choose to add an item photo, Girl Math It asks for permission to access your photo library. Selected photos are copied into the app's local storage on your device. They are not uploaded to Girl Math It servers.

## Data Collection

Girl Math It does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share your purchase data, item photos, or app usage data.

## Analytics and Advertising

Girl Math It does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

## Backups

Girl Math It does not currently provide cloud backup or server sync. Your data stays on your device unless you manually back it up using your device's normal backup tools.

## Children

Girl Math It is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes

Girl Math It is intended to remain local-first and private. If the app’s privacy practices ever change, this policy will be updated before release.
