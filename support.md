## Common Questions

### Is my data stored online?
No. Girl Math It stores your purchases and photos locally on your device.

### Do I need an account?
No. Girl Math It does not use accounts.

### Why did my item become "Basically Free"?
Girl Math It calculates cost per use. The more you use an item, the lower the per-use cost gets.

### How do I delete an item?
Swipe left on an item and tap Delete, or open the item and use Delete Purchase.
